Premier Class Services

Arrow Premier Class offers a dedicated chauffeur and vehicle for the utmost luxury service.

This service is catered for those who need individualized service based upon preferences and schedules.

Premier Class service includes:


Late model luxury vehicle
A dedicated chauffeur — clients may select their driver of choice
Amenities based upon the client’s preference (i.e., botttled water, newspaper)
Customized name sign for pick ups
A dedicated Arrow representative to assist in coordinating the day’s itinerary

Our standard sedan/SUV rate for this service is $80/hr (2 hr min.) + 20% gratuity. Please call us for pricing on specialty vehicles such as stretch limos and vans.
